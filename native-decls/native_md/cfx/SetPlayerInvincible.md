## SET_PLAYER_INVINCIBLE

```c
void SET_PLAYER_INVINCIBLE(Player player, BOOL bInvincible);
```

**This is the server-side RPC native equivalent of the client native [SET\_PLAYER\_INVINCIBLE](?_0x239528EACDC3E7DE).**
